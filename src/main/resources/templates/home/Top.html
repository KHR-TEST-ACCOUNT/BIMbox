Welcom to BIMbox!

"BIMbox" はシェアハウスでのコミュニティの活性化を目的とした掲示板型チャットサービスです。

About BIMbox

User Suggest

プロフィールに趣味を登録しておくと、コミュニティー内で共通点を持ってる人を探しその人物をサジェストします。

Search for a user

いつでも気になるユーザーの情報を検索する事ができ、自分と気が合いそうなユーザーを見つける事ができます。

Share idea, photo

アイデアや写真などをシェアする事ができ、コミュニティー内でのみ公開されます。（匿名投稿機能を追加予定です。）

1 on 1 chat

気になるユーザーとの１対１のチャットルームを作る事ができます。まずは趣味の合う人を見つけてメッセージを送ってみましょう。

Explore Norway

Ålesundis a town and municipality in Møre og Romsdal County, Norway. It is part of the traditional district of Sunnmøre and the centre of the Ålesund Region. It is a sea port and is noted for its concentration of Art Nouveau architecture. The town of Ålesund is the administrative centre of Ålesund Municipality, as well as the principal shipping town of the Sunnmøre district.

The 99-square-kilometre (38 sq mi) municipality is the 382nd largest by area out of the 422 municipalities in Norway. Ålesund is the 17th most populous municipality in Norway with a population of 47,199. The municipality's population density is 506.6 inhabitants per square kilometre (1,312/sq mi) and its population has increased by 14% over the last decade.

Explore Germany

Neuschwanstein Castleis a 19th-century Romanesque Revival palace on a rugged hill above the village of Hohenschwangau near Füssen in southwest Bavaria, Germany. The palace was commissioned by Ludwig II of Bavaria as a retreat and in honour of Richard Wagner. Ludwig paid for the palace out of his personal fortune and by means of extensive borrowing, rather than Bavarian public funds.

The castle was intended as a home for the king, until he died in 1886. It was open to the public shortly after his death. Since then more than 61 million people have visited Neuschwanstein Castle. More than 1.3 million people visit annually, with as many as 6,000 per day in the summer.
